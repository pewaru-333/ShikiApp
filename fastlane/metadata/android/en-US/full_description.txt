<b>Main functions at the moment</b>

* Search and view information about anime, manga and ranobe.
* Search and view information about characters and people.
* View the anime series release schedule.
* View and read news and topics.
* View user profiles, clubs, and friends.
* View your conversations and send messages to other users.
* Add anime, manga, and ranobe to your list and favorites.
* Add characters and people to favorites.
* The ability to join or leave clubs(s).
